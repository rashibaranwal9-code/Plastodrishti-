Plastodrishti 2026 is an Class 10 science project designed to solve the global microplastic tracking problem. By utilizing an ultra-affordable 15x magnification macro optical lens attachment clipped precisely over a standard smartphone camera sensor, our setup allows anyone to instantly scan water samples at home. The system illuminates the fluid matrix sample from beneath using an even white LED backlight stage. This specific alignment isolates microscopic plastic fibers down to micrometer thresholds, bypassing the need for heavy, expensive laboratory spectrometers.
