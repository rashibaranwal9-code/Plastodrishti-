Our Low-Cost Innovation

By combining affordable household materials with consumer mobile devices, we built an analytical tracking kit that bypasses thousands of dollars in scientific gear.

🔍

10x Macro Lens

An ultra-affordable smartphone clip lens attachment that magnifies water droplets significantly, allowing the phone sensor to visually capture micro-particles underneath 5mm.

📦

UV Isolation Box

A simple dark box setup lined with high-efficiency 365nm ultraviolet LED strips. It cuts down external ambient lighting errors completely to establish static test consistency.

🧪

Fluorescent Staining

Adsorbing fluorescent tracking dyes onto sample targets. The dye molecules selectively bond to plastics, causing them to vividly glow under specific light paths.
